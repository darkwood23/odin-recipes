Latte

Description

This latte is for all fellow coffee drinkers. I love cafe lattes but buying them can be pricey. So here's a recipe so you can make your own.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 1/3 cups hot freshly brewed dark roast espresso coffee

Steps

  1. Step 1: Heat milk in a saucepan set over medium-low heat. Whisk briskly with a wire whisk to create foam.
  2. Step 2: Brew espresso and pour into four cups. Pour in milk, holding back the foam with a spoon. Spoon foam over the top.
